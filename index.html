About Us

Clean Cow Tallow was started by Lyndsay, a stay-at-home mom of three seeking to simplify and detoxify her home and self-care products. After trying her hand at DIY tallow balm, she found that in addition to making a good product, she also enjoyed the process. Each jar of tallow is made in small batches, and packaged by hand. Every purchase you make supports a real family, and will help us fund necessary repairs to our home or family vehicle, or any other needs that come up. Thank you!
